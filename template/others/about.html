About Us

WHAT DRIVES US

Online book sharing and donating platform.

For the students in a developing country like Nepal, buying new books every single year may be a big challenge and may ruin their limited budget. After being done with the books they bought, they sell to someone without getting paid much or they just end up wasting the books. Most students are unaware for buying and selling the old books as there are very few platforms for buying and selling old books. So in this website allows users to buy, sell and donate the old books. With this application buyers can buy books at an affordable price, sellers can get paid what they deserve. Even the cool things about this web application is we can even help the needy students by donating our books..
